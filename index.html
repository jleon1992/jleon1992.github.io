Why am I creating this site?

This is my first official portfolio website. I was waiting until I became an expert web developer to create a website that I am proud of. While this is still my intention it has grown into something I can work on while I am on my quest to become a fully independent web developer. I am hoping to share this webpage with other fellow programmers on forums for support and inspiration. My final goal for this website is that it serves as a resume for future employers. I hope that through this website they will be able to see my work for exactly what it is. If done correctly this website will truly help employers make a decision as to whether I would be a valuable asset to their team, saving time and money for everybody.


I also intend on making this website fully interactive, and responsive, allowing anyone to contact me with any questions related to my work, programming, ideas, or anything else that is relevant.
